about

Thanks for dropping by, as you probably already know my name is Kendra Hallam.


I have spent the past 10 years as a Digital Production Artist at Universal Engraving, Inc. During my time at UEI, I gained knowledge creating textures, manipulating images and typography while using Illustrator & Photoshop. Prior to my experience at Universal Engraving Inc., I obtained a Bachelor of Science degree in Commercial Graphics from Pittsburg State University, in Pittsburg, Kansas. Following my bachelors degree, I began seeking a Master of Science in Technical Teacher Education. I guess you could say I can never get enough of education.


I have recently received certification through the University of Kansas for Full Stack Development. Let the journey begin...
